How we cooperate with you

We offer many different levels of services to meet the operational
and financial goals of our clients.

Team Ekstention Project-based Time & Materials Customized Partnership

Team extension (Outstaffing)

A fully dedicated remote team or person. Since the team might be working in places like New York or Kiev, we take care of the logistics. You just pay for labor.

Recommended for:

Companies who want to have just one contact for the duration of the project

Project-based (fixed price)

A fully dedicated remote team or person. Since the team might be working in places like New York or Kiev, we take care of the logistics. You just pay for labor.

Recommended for:

Classic model that guarantees the delivery of a predefined set of functionalities within a specific time frame.

Time & Materials

The client just pays only for development & design or consulting time

Recommended for:

Consultation purposes and/or to build an extension of your own teams to meet deadlines. For short term projects without clear definitions.

Customized Partnership

We are happy to explore other ways to be the right business partner for you. Book a consultation now! We are committed to giving you our best.

Recommended for:

Companies who have reached their break-even point, raised Round B+ or have a significant organic growth rate.
